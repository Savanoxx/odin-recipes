Description

Smoked Salmon is a recipe that is made with a smoker. Smoked Salmon is usually served with a sauce of some kind, accompanied by potatoes or any other side dish of choice. A classic dish that everyone is sure to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds salmon, trout or char
  • Birch or maple syrup for basting

Brine

  • 1 quart cool water
  • 2 ounces kosher salt
  • 1 cup brown sugar
