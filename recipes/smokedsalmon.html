Smoked Salmon

Description

Smoked Salmon is a recipe that is made with a smoker. Smoked Salmon is usually served with a sauce of some kind, accompanied by potatoes or any other side dish of choice. A classic dish that everyone is sure to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds salmon, trout or char
  • Birch or maple syrup for basting

Brine

  • 1 quart cool water
  • 2 ounces kosher salt
  • 1 cup brown sugar

Steps

  1. Mix brine, and place fish in plastic or a glass container
  2. Cure salmon for 4 hours, silver salmon needs 8 hours
  3. Rinse, and place on cooling rack, skin side down
  4. Let fish dry for 2-4 hours
  5. Place oil on skin, and place in smoker.
  6. 140-150 F for an hour, then 175F for an hour or two, depending on char
  7. When smoked, cool fish for an hour or put in fridge. Enjoy!
