Description

Lasagna, is a type of pasta, including very wide, flat sheets. In Italian cuisine it is made of stacked layers of pasta with fillings, often made with ground meat and tomato pasta of some kind.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • Teaspoon of salt, more to taste

Tomato sauce

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound sausage
  • Yellow onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning

Cheese Mixture

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg beaten
