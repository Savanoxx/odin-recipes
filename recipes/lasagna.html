Lasagna

Home

Description

Lasagna, is a type of pasta, including very wide, flat sheets. In Italian cuisine it is made of stacked layers of pasta with fillings, often made with ground meat and tomato pasta of some kind.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • Teaspoon of salt, more to taste

Tomato sauce

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound sausage
  • Yellow onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning

Cheese Mixture

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Add the lasagna noodles to salted water and boil
  3. Brown the beef, onion, sausage and garlic in a large skillet
  4. Stir the pasta sauce, tomato paste, italian seasoning, salt and pepper
  5. Combine mozzarella, parmesan, ricotta, parsley, egg and 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  6. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in a casserole dish. Top it with 3 lasagna noodles, layer 1/3 ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce.
  7. Repeat above steps twice
  8. Cover with foil and bake 45 min
  9. Remove foil and add rest of the remaining cheese
  10. Rest for 15 minutes, then enjoy!
