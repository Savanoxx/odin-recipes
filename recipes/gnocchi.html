Gnocchi

Home

Description

Gnocchi are a varied family of pasta-like dumplings in Italian cuisine. They are made of small rolls of dough, such as those composed of a simple combination of wheat flour, potato, egg and salt. Here is a recipe for it!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of gold potatoes
  • 1 2/3 to 2 cups of all purpose flour, and more as needed
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • Sauce for topping, such as pesto, tomato or meat

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes for 20 to 30 minutes depending on size.
  2. Drain and dry the potatoes and transfer to hot pan to cook.
  3. Let the potatoes cool, then peel.
  4. Make the gnocchi dough. Place flour onto table, process potato through a ricer or a strainer onto the flour.
  5. Roll the dough out into ropes.
  6. Cut the ropes to 1 inch pieces.
  7. Form the gnocchi.
  8. Boil the gnocchi on medium-high heat for 2 minutes.
  9. Top with sauce and serve.
